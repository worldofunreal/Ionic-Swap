Key Points

Ed25519 standard signatures with IC-compatible subkey derivation.

PrivateKey can be serialized (raw seed or PKCS8).

DerivedPrivateKey can sign, but cannot be serialized.

Paths (DerivationPath) mirror BIP32 or custom IC rules.

Errors (*DecodingError, SignatureError) are std::error::Error compatible.
